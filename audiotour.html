     Audio Tour
    Augmenting travel experience using audio | Side Project

    Summary: How many times have you travelled to a new place? How many times have you taken big bus tour?


    OBSERVATION

    Audio in Big Bus

    I was on a Big Bus tour when I realized that this is fun and informative experience but limited in scope- I can’t listen to audio if I am not on bus, it is only available for major metropolitan cities and it covers few major spots.



    INTERVEW

    How was your travel experience?

    I interviewed users to understand their travel experience? How did they go about exploring the city? What did they like about the experience and what they didn't. What will motivate them to share their story?


    ANALYSIS

    Personas


    Tasks

    Some important tasks were selected for MVP version of the app.



    CONCEPT

    Idea

    Location based audio tours by locals for everyone. People can create and experience location based audio tour of architecture tour, food crawl tour, bike tour, famous spots, flora/ fauna tour.


    User Flows for creating tour



    PROTOTYPE

    Task 1: Create a new tour

    As in scenario 2, you are Freddy who has just heard about the app and downloaded it. Now you want to make a audio tour about your food crawl in SF.


    Task 2: Go on a SF Food Crawl tour

    You are visiting SF from far and you have heard that SF has amazing places to eat but instead of sticking to one restaurant you want to try different restaurants. You just heard about this app and now you want to try it.
