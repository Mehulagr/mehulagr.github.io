Hey there!

I'm Mehul Agrawal, a UX Designer with a profound interest in the areas of Tangible User Interfaces and Design for Education. I acquired my bachelor's degree from DOD, IIT Guwahati after 4 fun-filled years of exploration in the deep waters of Design. I love to fiddle with code and I'd like to think it gives me the power to bring some of my designs to life, while also offering me with a completely different perspective on problems. I draw immense pleasure from tinkering, building and making things work and can be generally seen wielding a camera in one hand and a ping-pong paddle in the other.
