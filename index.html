About



Hola!

I am UX designer and researcher specializing in interaction design and rapid prototyping for emerging technologies. Currently a first year MS-HCID student at IUB with over 5 years of experience in design. From my explorations, I have learnt that the secret sauce for a good design solution is to marry right technology with right users with an hint of pleasure and delight. Its not only important to integrate users perspective into design, but also of other stakeholders like developers and managers.

I love exploring and routinely study different applications and their design choices. Playing with code or a good action thriller keep me awake late nights and design discussions leave me intrigued. I appreciate simplicity in work, life and definitely in my cup of coffee.
