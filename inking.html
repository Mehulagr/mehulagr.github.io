    Inking

    Pen Gestures for augmenting digital writing experience and capabilities

    Brief

    Although the existing inking and notetaking apps for tablet like evernote, one note etc. powerfull, but they do not harness all the capabilities of digital medium. Features in these app are limited to most common functions like cut, copy, paste, undo redo stroke colour and width change, etc. But digital medium is very powerful and can do many things like perform calculations, visualize, search etc. We are trying to provide these features in a note taking app. In the app we propose, these wide array of functions have common umbrella of accessing them i.e. pen based gestures/ keywords.

    Introduction

    Design an app for touch devices that uses Inking (Pen based input on digital canvas) as the primary way of input and creation.


    Secondary Study

    Compartivie study helped in understanding the present market of inking applications. They helped us in answering questions like- What all is already exsisting? What are most common features in inking applications? and many more. Simultaneously, a study of research papers helped us in defining some problems that users face while using inking applications and their solutions.


    Primary Study

    Users were questioned to understand their exsisting inking patterns and needs. Questions for user study encompassed all three aspects of inking i.e. notes, diagrams and drawings. Ten users were interviewed in the process. Some important points that emerged out of the interviews were-
    Making side notes, “underlining” important points and marking content for later review using different symbols, markers etc.
    Using other tools like calculators, convertors, graphs, etc.

    User Interviews Analysis

    Further, an activity was conducted to get understanding of interaction patterns when users interacted with the paper and when users interacted with the tablet. One major observation during activities was use of non-preferred hand for interacting with paper (using one hand to move (reposition) the paper and other to write on paper). Two different activities were undertaken by 5 users each.

    Design Statement and Persona

    Design interactions for a digital note taking application which leverages the experience of paper and the power of digital medium to create more productive content for college students.

    Product and Prototype

    How many additional features does evernote provide as compared to paper? The current app like evernote, one note etc. have only few features like cut, copy, paste, undo redo stroke colour and width change, etc. But digital medium is very powerful and can do many things like perform calculations, visualize, search etc. We are trying to provide these features using a note taking app. These functions have common umbrella of accessing them i.e. pen based gestures/ keywords. A prototype with animation and gestures was designed using powerpoint.

    Novelty of the app lies in the way user interacts with the system to perform common tasks. The gestures are adopted from the daily behavior of user while taking notes.
