    Mavenly: Design Strategy
    Leveraging brand community for establishing better brand engagement and influencers

    • Sep'16 (3 weeks)

    • Group of 3

    • For: Mavenly

    • Slide Show

    Mavenly is an influencer marketing startup with vision to have a humanized influencer marketing platform. For this project they were looking for a strategic design to scale it up and productize it, targeting millennials. In influencer marketing "Rather than marketing directly to a large group of consumers, you instead inspire / hire / pay influencers to get out the word for you." These influencers can be anyone from big influencers like Selena Gomez to small bloggers or active social media users. Mavenly calls the brand influencers, advocates or ambassadors as Mavens.


    92% of consumers around the world say they trust recommendations from friends and family above all other forms of advertising.

    Market Study and SWOT

    What is unique about Mavenly and where do competitors lack?
    A lot of influencer platforms are oriented around establishing a marketplace that looks more like Amazon where you can buy influencers. But this method of selling influencers dehumanizes the process of influencing marketing and decreases authenticity and thereby effectiveness of the process.




    User Research

    Why do millennials post about a brand/ product on social media?
    We interviewed 20 college students to understand their relationships with brands and their perspective on sponsored posts. Millennials want to feel valuable to brands and help shape products, especially when incentivized. Long-term, positive brand-customer interactions turn customers into vocal brand advocates.




    actively follow brands of their interests


    want to shape products by providing feedback


    strong relation with brand increases brand advocacy


    Value Proposition
    How do you organically inspire people to write post about your brand in social media?


    User Need

    Brand Need
    Feedback to strengthen products and marketing strategies
    Brand Engagement
    Brand Advocacy

    Ideation

    What does it take to establish loyal communities for brands?
    We found that establishing strong brand community with positive relationship was a way to convert people into loyal brand advocates. Effectively productize and scale the process of building and managing Mavens by establishing stronger brand community using our platform.


    Brand Awareness → Brand Advocates
    We are trying to leverage increase brand engagement through community.
